### **2.G. Official OCR Mark Scheme Descriptors (June 2024\)**

**Internal AI Note:** Use these official OCR level descriptors to align feedback with exam board expectations. Reference specific band characteristics when explaining marks awarded or improvements needed.

#### **Level 6 (31-36 marks): Sustained critical style in an informed personal response**

**AO1:** Coherent critical style sustained in an informed personal response to the text, showing consistently perceptive understanding.

**AO2:** Detailed and well-developed analysis of writer's use of language, form and structure to create meanings and effects. Consistently effective use of relevant subject terminology.

**AO3:** Perceptive and sensitive understanding of context and how it informs evaluation of the text.

**Typical features:** Textual references and quotations are precise, pertinent and skilfully interwoven. At the top of this level, responses demonstrate sustained critical style with consistently perceptive understanding throughout.

#### **Level 5 (25-30 marks): Convincing critical style in a well-developed personal response**

**AO1:** Convincing critical style maintained in a well-developed personal response to the text, showing some insightful understanding.

**AO2:** Thoughtful and developed analysis of writer's use of language, form and structure to create meanings and effects. Good use of relevant subject terminology.

**AO3:** Convincing understanding of context which informs the response to the text.

**Typical features:** Textual references and quotations are well-selected and fully integrated. At the top of this level, responses demonstrate convincing critical style with thoughtful development throughout.

#### **Level 4 (19-24 marks): Credible critical style in a detailed personal response**

**AO1:** Some critical style demonstrated in a detailed personal response to the text, showing clear understanding.

**AO2:** Some analysis of writer's use of language, form and structure to create meanings and effects. Competent use of relevant subject terminology.

**AO3:** Clear understanding of context which informs the response to the text.

**Typical features:** Relevant textual references and quotations are selected to support the response. At the top of this level, responses demonstrate credible critical style with clear understanding throughout.

#### **Level 3 (13-18 marks): A reasonably developed personal response**

**AO1:** Reasonably detailed personal response to the text showing understanding.

**AO2:** Reasonable explanation of writer's use of language, form and structure to create meanings and effects. Some use of relevant subject terminology.

**AO3:** Some relevant comments about context inform the response to the text.

**Typical features:** Some use of relevant textual references and quotations to support the response. At the top of this level, responses demonstrate reasonable development with sustained understanding.

#### **Level 2 (7-12 marks): A straightforward personal response**

**AO1:** Begins to develop a straightforward personal response to the text showing some understanding.

**AO2:** Simple comments on writer's use of language, form or structure. Limited use of subject terminology.

**AO3:** Some awareness of context, which may be implied.

**Typical features:** Some relevant support from the text. At the top of this level, responses begin to develop straightforward personal response with emerging understanding.

#### **Level 1 (1-6 marks): A basic response**

**AO1:** Makes a few relevant comments about the text. Makes limited references to the text.

**AO2:** A little awareness of language, form or structure. Very little use of subject terminology.

**AO3:** A little awareness of context implied, related to the text.

**Typical features:** At the top of this level, responses demonstrate basic engagement with the text and task.
