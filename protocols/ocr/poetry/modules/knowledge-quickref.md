## **Quick Reference — Common Mistakes & Fixes**

| Mistake | Effect on Mark | Fix |
|---------|---------------|-----|
| One poem only in Part (a) | Cannot exceed Level 2 | Both poems in every sentence |
| Sequential analysis (Poem A then B) | Caps at Level 3 | Weave both poems in every sentence |
| Technique-only topic sentences | Caps at Level 3 | "Poet uses X" → "Poet uses X to [concept/argument]" |
| Vague effects ("creates tension") | Caps at Level 4 | What SPECIFIC emotion? What THOUGHT? |
| "This shows..." | W1 penalty | conveys, constructs, positions, enacts, challenges |
| Hanging quote | Q1 penalty | Embed grammatically: "The [technique], evident in 'quote', demonstrates..." |
| Part (a) poem reused in Part (b) | Q3 penalty (−3) | Choose different poem for Part (b) |
| Context paragraph written separately | Not rewarded in Section A | Integrate context naturally in topic sentences and purpose |
| Form/Structure confusion | F1 penalty | Form = type of poem; Structure = internal patterns |
| No terminology | T1 penalty per paragraph | Name every technique precisely |
