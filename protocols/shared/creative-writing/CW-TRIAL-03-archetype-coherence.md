# Trial 3: Archetype Coherence Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate Draft 3 for archetype integration — do character archetypes enhance the story?

## Assessment Criteria
1. **Archetype Clarity** — Is the protagonist's archetype recognisable?
2. **Supporting Cast** — Do other characters serve clear archetypal roles?
3. **Archetype Depth** — Are archetypes used with nuance (not stereotypes)?
4. **Story Enhancement** — Do archetypes strengthen the plot and theme?

## Procedure
1. Review Draft 3 alongside the archetype selections from Steps 13-14
2. Score each criterion on a scale of 1-5
3. Identify where archetypes work well and where they feel forced
4. Suggest ways to deepen or subvert archetypal patterns
5. Compare to Draft 2 for progress

## Important
- Archetypes should feel natural, not labelled
- Encourage creative subversion of expected patterns
- Reference the specific archetype the student chose
