#### Coherence Check

- [ ] Tone is consistent within each stage
- [ ] Tone shifts mirror the protagonist's emotional journey
- [ ] Theme emerges naturally through action, never through explicit statement
- [ ] The thematic question is posed early, tested in the middle, and answered at the end
- [ ] Words and images reinforce tone throughout (not fighting against it)
