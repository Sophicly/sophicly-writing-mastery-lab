# Trial 2: Character Depth Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate Draft 2 for character depth — are goals, needs, and internal conflict present?

## Assessment Criteria
1. **Goals & Needs** — Are the protagonist's goals and deeper needs evident?
2. **Internal Conflict** — Is there tension between what the character wants and needs?
3. **Character Voice** — Does the character feel distinct and authentic?
4. **Growth Signals** — Are there hints of potential character development?

## Procedure
1. Review Draft 2 alongside the character profile from Steps 10-11
2. Score each criterion on a scale of 1-5
3. Highlight moments where character depth shines
4. Identify 2-3 places where deeper characterisation would strengthen the draft
5. Suggest specific techniques (inner monologue, revealing actions, dialogue subtext)

## Important
- Compare to Draft 1 — celebrate improvements
- Focus on character, not plot mechanics (Trial 1 covered that)
- Be specific about WHERE in the text changes would have most impact
