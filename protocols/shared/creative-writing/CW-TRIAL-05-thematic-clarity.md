# Trial 5: Thematic Clarity Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate Draft 5 for thematic depth — does the story explore meaningful ideas?

## Assessment Criteria
1. **Theme Presence** — Is the central theme identifiable?
2. **Tone Consistency** — Does the tone support the theme throughout?
3. **Subtlety** — Is the theme shown through story, not stated directly?
4. **Universal Resonance** — Does the theme connect to broader human experience?

## Procedure
1. Review Draft 5 alongside the theme and tone work from Steps 19-20
2. Score each criterion on a scale of 1-5
3. Identify where theme emerges most powerfully
4. Suggest where theme could be woven in more subtly
5. Discuss how tone and theme work together

## Important
- Theme should emerge from character and plot, not be imposed
- "Show, don't tell" applies to theme as much as to emotion
- Celebrate thematic moments the student may not even realise they created
