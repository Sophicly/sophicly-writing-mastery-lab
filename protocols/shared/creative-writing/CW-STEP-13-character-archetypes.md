#### Concept: Character Archetypes

Archetypes are universal character patterns recognised across all cultures. Your protagonist will shift between archetypes as they transform. This shift IS the visible evidence of their change.

**The 9 Core Archetypes:**

| Archetype | Traits | Effect on Reader |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| **The Everyman** | Common name, job, home, family | Empathy — we see ourselves in them |
| **The Underdog** | Small size, odds stacked against them, lack of self-esteem | Compassion, admiration, suspense |
| **The Orphan** | Independent, cynical, pragmatic, vulnerable, loyal | Empathy, identification, inspiration |
| **The Lost Soul** | Outsider, emotionally wounded, hiding from community | Warning, understanding |
| **The Rebel** | Outspoken, radical, motivates others against injustice | Admiration, reflects our desire for change |
| **The Explorer** | Outgoing, seeks new experiences, rejects boundaries | Represents stepping outside comfort zones |
| **The Mentor** | Knowledgeable, wise, guides others, has faith in good | Respect, demonstrates solutions |
| **The Trickster** | Mischievous, disruptive, cunning, cuts egos down to size | Comic relief, fresh perspective |
| **The Shadow** | Destructive, frightening, challenges good | Reflects fears and trauma |
| **The Hero** | Courageous, skilled, self-sacrificing, ambitious | Admiration, aspiration |
