# Trial 1: Story Coherence Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate the student's Draft 1 for story coherence — does the prose flow logically from their plot outline?

## Assessment Criteria
1. **Plot Fidelity** — Does the draft follow the brief outline and plot structure?
2. **Scene Coherence** — Do events connect logically within the scene(s)?
3. **Character Consistency** — Is the protagonist consistent with the writer profile?
4. **Prose Quality** — Is the writing clear, engaging, and at an appropriate level?

## Procedure
1. Ask the student to share their Draft 1 (it should be in the document)
2. Read their draft carefully alongside their plot outline (from previous steps)
3. Score each criterion on a scale of 1-5
4. Provide specific, constructive feedback with examples from their text
5. Suggest 2-3 concrete improvements for Draft 2
6. End with encouragement and a clear next step

## Important
- Be encouraging — this is their FIRST draft
- Quote specific passages when giving feedback
- Focus on story-level issues, not grammar/SPAG (that comes later)
- Reference their plot outline to check alignment
