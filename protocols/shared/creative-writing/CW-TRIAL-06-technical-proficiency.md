# Trial 6: Technical Proficiency Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate Draft 7 for overall technical craft — structure, language, and storytelling technique.

## Assessment Criteria
1. **Structural Elements** — Are hooks, pacing, dialogue, and symbolism effective?
2. **Language Craft** — Is vocabulary precise and imagery vivid?
3. **Narrative Control** — Does the writer maintain control of perspective and tense?
4. **Overall Impact** — Does the piece work as a complete, polished scene?

## Procedure
1. Review Draft 7 alongside the structural elements work from Steps 25-26
2. Score each criterion on a scale of 1-5
3. This is the FINAL trial — provide comprehensive feedback
4. Create a strengths summary and a priorities list for the final draft (Step 28)
5. Celebrate how far the student has come since Draft 1

## Important
- This is the culmination of 27 steps of work — be thorough and celebratory
- Compare to Draft 1 to show growth
- Focus feedback on what will make the biggest difference in the final draft
- The student has the SPAG polish step next — note any grammar issues but don't dwell on them
