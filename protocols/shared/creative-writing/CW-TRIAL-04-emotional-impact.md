# Trial 4: Emotional Impact Assessment

## Purpose
Evaluate Draft 4 for emotional engagement — does the reader feel something?

## Assessment Criteria
1. **Empathy Techniques** — Are show-don't-tell, sensory detail, and vulnerability used?
2. **Emotional Arc** — Does the scene build emotional intensity?
3. **Reader Connection** — Does the reader care about the character's experience?
4. **Authenticity** — Do emotions feel genuine rather than melodramatic?

## Procedure
1. Review Draft 4 alongside the empathy plan from Steps 16-17
2. Score each criterion on a scale of 1-5
3. Identify the strongest emotional moment and explain why it works
4. Suggest 2-3 places where emotional depth could be added
5. Recommend specific empathy techniques for those moments

## Important
- Emotional writing is vulnerable — be especially encouraging
- Quote the passages that moved you most
- Focus on technique, not just content
