# Great Expectations — Key Quotes for Mark Scheme Examples

- "I loved her against reason, against promise, against peace, against hope, against happiness, against all discouragement that could be" (Chapter 29) — Theme: Obsessive love, self-destruction, social aspiration
- "I am what you designed me to be. I am your pupil" (Chapter 38) — Theme: Manipulation, responsibility, identity
- "Suffering has been stronger than all other teaching" (Chapter 59) — Theme: Growth through pain, redemption, maturity
- "He calls the knaves, Jacks, this boy!" (Chapter 8) — Theme: Class, snobbery, shame
- "In a word, I was too cowardly to do what I knew to be right, as I had been too cowardly to avoid doing what I knew to be wrong" (Chapter 6) — Theme: Moral cowardice, guilt, social pressure
- "I saw that the bride within the bridal dress had withered like the dress" (Chapter 8) — Theme: Decay, time, the cost of bitterness
