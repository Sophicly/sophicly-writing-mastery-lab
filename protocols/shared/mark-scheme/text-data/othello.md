# Othello — Key Quotes for Mark Scheme Examples

- "I am not what I am" (Act 1, Scene 1) — Theme: Deception, duplicity, identity
- "O, beware, my lord, of jealousy; / It is the green-eyed monster which doth mock the meat it feeds on" (Act 3, Scene 3) — Theme: Jealousy, manipulation, dramatic irony
- "Put out the light, and then put out the light" (Act 5, Scene 2) — Theme: Death, irreversibility, tragic resolve
- "Speak of me as I am; nothing extenuate" (Act 5, Scene 2) — Theme: Identity, reputation, self-awareness
- "An old black ram / Is tupping your white ewe" (Act 1, Scene 1) — Theme: Racism, animalistic imagery, prejudice
- "She loved me for the dangers I had passed, / And I loved her that she did pity them" (Act 1, Scene 3) — Theme: Love, storytelling, mutual attraction
- "Reputation, reputation, reputation! O, I have lost my reputation!" (Act 2, Scene 3) — Theme: Honour, social standing, manipulation
