# The Merchant of Venice — Key Quotes for Mark Scheme Examples

- "If you prick us, do we not bleed? If you tickle us, do we not laugh?" (Act 3, Scene 1) — Theme: Prejudice, shared humanity, justice
- "The quality of mercy is not strained; / It droppeth as the gentle rain from heaven" (Act 4, Scene 1) — Theme: Mercy vs justice, Christian values, power
- "All that glisters is not gold" (Act 2, Scene 7) — Theme: Appearance vs reality, superficiality, wisdom
- "I am a Jew. Hath not a Jew eyes?" (Act 3, Scene 1) — Theme: Prejudice, identity, empathy
- "In sooth, I know not why I am so sad" (Act 1, Scene 1) — Theme: Melancholy, isolation, foreshadowing
- "The devil can cite Scripture for his purpose" (Act 1, Scene 3) — Theme: Hypocrisy, manipulation, religious prejudice
