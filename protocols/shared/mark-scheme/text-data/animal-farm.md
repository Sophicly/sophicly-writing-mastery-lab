# Animal Farm — Key Quotes for Mark Scheme Examples

- "All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others" (Chapter 10) — Theme: Corruption of ideals, hypocrisy, totalitarianism
- "Four legs good, two legs bad" (Chapter 3) — Theme: Propaganda, simplification, indoctrination
- "The creatures outside looked from pig to man, and from man to pig, and from pig to man again; but already it was impossible to say which was which" (Chapter 10) — Theme: Corruption, revolution betrayed, cyclical oppression
- "Napoleon is always right" (Chapter 5) — Theme: Blind loyalty, propaganda, authoritarianism
- "I will work harder" (Chapter 3) — Theme: Exploited loyalty, working class, naivety
- "No animal shall sleep in a bed — with sheets" (Chapter 6) — Theme: Corruption of law, manipulation of language, power
