# The Tempest — Key Quotes for Mark Scheme Examples

- "O brave new world, / That has such people in't!" (Act 5, Scene 1) — Theme: Innocence, wonder, irony
- "We are such stuff / As dreams are made on, and our little life / Is rounded with a sleep" (Act 4, Scene 1) — Theme: Illusion vs reality, mortality, the ephemeral
- "Hell is empty and all the devils are here" (Act 1, Scene 2) — Theme: Chaos, colonialism, supernatural power
- "This thing of darkness I / Acknowledge mine" (Act 5, Scene 1) — Theme: Responsibility, colonialism, self-awareness
- "Full fathom five thy father lies; / Of his bones are coral made" (Act 1, Scene 2) — Theme: Transformation, death, magic
- "Be not afeard; the isle is full of noises, / Sounds and sweet airs, that give delight and hurt not" (Act 3, Scene 2) — Theme: Nature, beauty, Caliban's humanity
