# Twelfth Night — Key Quotes for Mark Scheme Examples

- "If music be the food of love, play on" (Act 1, Scene 1) — Theme: Love, excess, melancholy
- "Some are born great, some achieve greatness, and some have greatness thrust upon 'em" (Act 2, Scene 5) — Theme: Ambition, self-deception, social class
- "Make me a willow cabin at your gate, / And call upon my soul within the house" (Act 1, Scene 5) — Theme: Unrequited love, devotion, performance
- "I am not what I am" (Act 3, Scene 1) — Theme: Disguise, identity, gender
- "Better a witty fool than a foolish wit" (Act 1, Scene 5) — Theme: Wisdom, folly, appearance vs reality
- "Love sought is good, but given unsought, is better" (Act 3, Scene 1) — Theme: Love, fate, natural affection
