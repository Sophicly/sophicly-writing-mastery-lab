- "Ralph wept for the end of innocence, the darkness of man's heart" (Chapter 12) — Theme: Loss of innocence, civilisation vs savagery, grief
- "The rules are the only thing we've got!" (Chapter 5) — Theme: Order, democracy, civilisation
- "What are we? Humans? Or animals? Or savages?" (Chapter 5) — Theme: Identity, civilisation vs savagery, moral choice
- "The thing is — fear can't hurt you any more than a dream" (Chapter 5) — Theme: Rationality, fear, the failure of reason
- "Roger gathered a handful of stones and began to throw them" (Chapter 4) — Theme: Cruelty, the erosion of civilisation, foreshadowing
