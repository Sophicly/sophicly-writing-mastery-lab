## Student Recommendation

**Examiner favourites:**
- Social class/responsibility — asked 3 times, the dominant theme (same as AQA)
- The Inspector — featured in 2 papers, Edexcel values his dramatic function
- Birling — features heavily as the embodiment of capitalist selfishness

**Key Edexcel difference:** Edexcel provides an EXTRACT. Students must analyse the given passage AND the wider play. AQA gives no extract.

**Strong prediction candidates (never asked on Edexcel):**
- Eric — never the direct focus despite being central to the plot
- Mrs Birling — never asked, rich for class and gender analysis
- Gerald — never the focus, his relationship with Eva/Daisy is complex
- The ending/twist — the telephone ring, dramatic irony, never asked
- Gender/women — never asked on Edexcel, increasingly popular on other boards
