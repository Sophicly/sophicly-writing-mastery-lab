## Student Recommendation

**Examiner favourites (practice these FIRST):**
1. **Lady Macbeth** — appears in 5/7 extracts. She is AQA's favourite character for this text
2. **Act 1 Scene 7** — used TWICE. Know this scene inside out
3. **Ambition and Power** — asked twice, the play's central theme
4. **Guilt** — asked once but Lady Macbeth's guilt is implied in multiple papers

**Extract pattern:** AQA strongly prefers early play extracts (Act 1 = 4/7 papers). They want students to write about the WHOLE play from an early starting point.

**Strong prediction candidates:**
- **"Is this a dagger" soliloquy** (Act 2 Scene 1) — iconic scene, never used
- **Banquo** as a character — never been the focus despite being central to guilt/supernatural
- **The witches specifically** — supernatural asked (2023) but witches as characters never the direct focus
- **Act 5 Scene 5 "Tomorrow and tomorrow"** — Macbeth's final despair, never used
- **Fate vs free will** — central philosophical question of the play, never directly asked
