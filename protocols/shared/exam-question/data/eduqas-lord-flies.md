## EDUQAS Format Note

EDUQAS Section A (Post-1914) gives NO extract. Students write from memory about the character/theme "at different points in the novel."

## Student Recommendation

**Pattern:** EDUQAS rotates through the main characters. All four major boys have been asked about. Theme questions are rarer.

**Strong prediction candidates (never asked on EDUQAS):**
- Roger — the truly evil character, never the focus
- The Beast — central symbol of fear and the darkness within, never asked directly
- Civilisation vs savagery — the novel's central thesis, only obliquely asked (2019)
- The killings (Simon and Piggy) — the climactic events, never the direct focus
- Power and leadership — asked via character but never as a direct theme question
