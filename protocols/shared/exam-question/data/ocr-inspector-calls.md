## OCR Format Note

OCR provides an extract for modern texts (unlike AQA which gives no extract for AIC). Students must analyse the extract AND the wider play.

## Student Recommendation

**Examiner favourites:**
- Social class/responsibility — asked twice, the universal favourite across all boards
- Character studies — OCR rotates through the cast like EDUQAS
- Gender — asked in 2023, an emerging trend across boards

**Strong prediction candidates (never asked on OCR):**
- Eric — never the focus despite being central to Act 3
- Gerald — never asked, his privileged position is rich for class analysis
- The Inspector as character — never the direct OCR focus
- Mrs Birling as sole focus — she appears in the class question but never alone
- The ending / dramatic irony — OCR loves dramatic irony (see Mr Birling 2022) but never asked about the twist
