# Topic 1: Ralph (Civilisation)
**Type:** A-MT
**Teaching Point:** Ralph represents democratic order and the hope of rescue. Students should explore how Golding uses Ralph to examine whether civilisation is natural or imposed, and how his authority erodes as the boys descend into savagery.
**Marks:** 40
**AOs:** AO1,AO2,AO3,AO4
**AO4:** 8
**Quote Prompt:** Ralph: "We've got to have rules and obey them. After all, we're not savages."

## Question
How does Golding present Ralph as a symbol of civilisation and order in *Lord of the Flies*? You must refer to the context of the novel in your answer.
