# Topic 1: Diagnostic — The Danger of a Single Story (Chimamanda Ngozi Adichie)
**Type:** single
**Format:** single
**Teaching Point:** Adichie's adapted TED Talk explores how single narratives reduce complex people and cultures to simplified stereotypes.
