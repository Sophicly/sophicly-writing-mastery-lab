# Topic 1: Diagnostic Paper — City of the Beasts (Isabel Allende)
**Type:** language_paper
**Format:** multi_question
**Source:** edexcel-igcse-language-p2.md, Topic 1
**Teaching Point:** Edexcel IGCSE Language Spec A Paper 2 — Poetry and Prose Texts and Imaginative Writing. This is the diagnostic assessment: the student's baseline before any teaching. Section A: one 30-mark analytical essay on a prose extract (AO1 + AO2). Section B: one 30-mark writing task chosen from three options (AO4 + AO5). Total: 60 marks. 1 hour 30 minutes.
**Marks:** 60
**AOs:** AO1, AO2, AO4, AO5
**Assessment Type:** diagnostic

# Topic 2: Conceptual Notes
**Type:** notes
**Teaching Point:** Key concepts for Edexcel IGCSE Language Spec A Paper 2 — prose analysis and imaginative writing skills, assessment objectives, question types, timing strategy, and techniques for each section. Also covers the creative writing course foundations: the Three Wells of inspiration, logline construction, Pixar Story Spine, the eight archetypal plot structures, character arc theory, and the Context-Concept-Technique principle.
