# Topic 1: Diagnostic Paper — Descriptive and Argumentative Writing
**Type:** language_paper
**Format:** multi_question
**Teaching Point:** Cambridge IGCSE Language Paper 2 Diagnostic — Writing. Two writing tasks, 50 marks total. Q1 tests descriptive or narrative writing (25 marks), Q2 tests argumentative, discursive, or persuasive writing (25 marks). No source texts — this is a pure writing paper. Phase 1 (first draft) establishes your baseline; Phase 2 (redraft) targets specific improvements identified in assessment.
**Marks:** 50
**AOs:** AO5, AO6

## Q1
**Marks:** 25 (13 content AO5 + 12 technical accuracy AO6)
**AOs:** AO5, AO6

Choose one of the following tasks:

**Either:**

(a) Write a description of a place that is transformed by extreme weather.

**Or:**

(b) Write a narrative that begins with the sentence: "The silence that morning was different — heavier, as if the world itself was holding its breath."

## Q2
**Marks:** 25 (13 content AO5 + 12 technical accuracy AO6)
**AOs:** AO5, AO6

Choose one of the following tasks:

**Either:**

(a) "The natural world is something to be feared, not admired." Write an article for a magazine in which you argue for or against this statement.

**Or:**

(b) "Young people should spend more time outdoors and less time on screens." Write a speech for a school assembly in which you give your views on this topic.
