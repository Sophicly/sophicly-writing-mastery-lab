### Step 4: How the Theme Creates Meaning

"Now for the key question: What MEANING does exploring [primary theme] create?

Think about:
- What does [the author] want us to UNDERSTAND about [theme]?
- How does this theme connect to [historical context]?
- What does [the author] want us to FEEL about [theme]?
- What might [the author] want us to DO or THINK differently because of this theme?

Complete this: 'Through the theme of [theme], [the author] **perhaps creates** the meaning that [statement about human experience/society/morality].' **Use tentative language** when discussing what the author aims to convey.

**Provide a quote that captures the MEANING [author] creates through this theme.**"
