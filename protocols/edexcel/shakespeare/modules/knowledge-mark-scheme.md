- Body Paragraph 3: 5 marks (TTECEA structure)

- **Total: 20 marks** (AO1 15 marks \+ AO3 5 marks)

**Note on Assessment Objectives:**

- **Q1(a):** 20 marks total (AO2 only \- analysis of language, form, structure)  
- **Q1(b):** 20 marks total (AO1 15 marks \- knowledge, understanding, engagement \+ AO3 5 marks \- context)

#### **Level 5: Assured (16-20 marks)**

**For Q1(a) \- AO2 Only:**

- Cohesive evaluation of language, form and structure  
- Discriminating use of relevant examples in support  
- Perceptive analysis of writer's methods

**For Q1(b) \- AO1 \+ AO3:**

- Assured knowledge and understanding of the text (AO1)  
- The response shows assured personal engagement and a perceptive critical style (AO1)  
- Discriminating use of relevant examples in support (AO1)  
- Thoughtful consideration of contextual factors and their influence on the text (AO3)

**Typical features:** At the top of this level, responses demonstrate mastery with perceptive insights, sophisticated analysis, and seamless integration of textual evidence. The critical voice is assured and engagement is highly informed.

#### **Level 4: Thorough (11-15 marks)**

**For Q1(a) \- AO2 Only:**

- Sustained analysis of language, form and structure  
- Use of fully relevant examples in support  
- Detailed analytical commentary on writer's methods

**For Q1(b) \- AO1 \+ AO3:**

- Thorough knowledge and understanding of the text (AO1)  
- The response shows thorough personal engagement and a sustained critical style (AO1)  
- Use of fully relevant examples in support (AO1)  
- Clear awareness of contextual factors and their relevance to the text (AO3)

**Typical features:** Responses demonstrate consistent strength with detailed textual knowledge, sustained analytical commentary, and well-chosen supporting evidence throughout.

#### **Level 3: Sound (7-10 marks)**

**For Q1(a) \- AO2 Only:**

- Sound understanding of language, form and structure  
- Use of clearly relevant examples in support  
- Clear analysis of writer's methods

**For Q1(b) \- AO1 \+ AO3:**

- Sound knowledge and understanding of the text (AO1)  
- The response shows relevant personal engagement and an appropriate critical style (AO1)  
- Use of clearly relevant examples in support (AO1)  
- Some awareness of contextual factors (AO3)

**Typical features:** Responses show secure understanding with clear analysis. Personal engagement is evident and examples are appropriately selected to support interpretation.

#### **Level 2: Some (4-6 marks)**

**For Q1(a) \- AO2 Only:**

- Some comment on the language, form and structure  
- Some use of relevant examples in support  
- Emerging analytical commentary

**For Q1(b) \- AO1 \+ AO3:**

- Some knowledge and understanding of the text (AO1)  
- The response may be largely narrative with some evidence of personal engagement or critical style (AO1)  
- Some use of relevant examples in support (AO1)  
- Limited awareness of contextual factors (AO3)

**Typical features:** Responses demonstrate partial understanding with some relevant points. May include narrative retelling alongside analytical comments. Evidence of engagement emerging but not yet sustained.

#### **Level 1: Limited (1-3 marks)**

**For Q1(a) \- AO2 Only:**

- Minimal identification of language, form and structure  
- Limited use of relevant examples in support  
- Basic observations about writer's methods

**For Q1(b) \- AO1 \+ AO3:**

- Limited knowledge and understanding of the text (AO1)  
- The response is simple with little evidence of personal engagement or critical style (AO1)  
- Limited use of relevant examples in support (AO1)  
- Minimal or no awareness of contextual factors (AO3)

**Typical features:** Responses show basic awareness with simple observations. Limited analytical engagement. May focus on surface-level content rather than deeper meanings or authorial choices.
