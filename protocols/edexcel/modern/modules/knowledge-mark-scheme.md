### **2.G. Official Edexcel GCSE Mark Scheme Descriptors — Modern Drama & Prose**

**Internal AI Note:** For Edexcel Paper 1, Section B (Modern Drama & Prose):

**Modern Text Question (34 marks total):**

- Introduction: 2 marks (hook + building sentences focusing on contextual analysis + thesis)
- Body Paragraph 1: 8 marks (TTECEA structure)
- Body Paragraph 2: 8 marks (TTECEA structure)
- Body Paragraph 3: 8 marks (TTECEA structure)
- Conclusion: 4 marks (Restated Thesis 0.5 + Controlling Concept 1.5 + Concept→Context Links 0.5 + Author's Purpose 1.5)
- **Total: 40 marks** (AO1 + AO3, with AO4 marks absorbed into section criteria. SPaG handled through penalty codes.)

**CRITICAL: No extract.** Modern Drama & Prose questions are ALWAYS whole-text. Evidence must come from across the entire text (beginning, middle, and end).

**Question Format:** "How does [author] use the character of [X] to explore ideas about [theme]?" or "How does [author] present ideas about [theme/concept] in [text]?"

**Note on Assessment Objectives:**
- **AO1**: Read, understand and respond to texts — maintaining a critical style, developing an informed personal response, using textual references including quotations
- **AO3**: Demonstrate understanding of the relationships between texts and the contexts in which they were written
- AO4 marks are absorbed into AO1+AO3 section criteria. SPaG quality is handled through penalty codes (G1, H1, P1).

Use the level descriptors below to align feedback with exam board expectations.

#### **Level 5: Assured (26-30 marks for AO1+AO3)**

- **AO1:** Critical, exploratory, conceptual analysis showing genuine engagement with the writer's methods and meanings. Judicious use of precise textual references integrated naturally into argument.
- **AO3:** Excellent understanding of context — sophisticated exploration of how context shapes meaning, with contextual factors woven throughout analysis rather than bolted on.

#### **Level 4: Thoughtful (20-25 marks for AO1+AO3)**

- **AO1:** Thoughtful, developed response showing clear personal engagement. Apt textual references support a coherent argument with some conceptual depth.
- **AO3:** Clear understanding of context — effective connections between text and context that enhance interpretation.

#### **Level 3: Clear (14-19 marks for AO1+AO3)**

- **AO1:** Clear, relevant response with some personal engagement. Textual references support the argument, though analysis may be uneven.
- **AO3:** Reasonable understanding of context — relevant contextual connections, though they may feel separate from analysis.

#### **Level 2: Supported (8-13 marks for AO1+AO3)**

- **AO1:** Supported response showing some understanding. Some relevant textual references, though may rely on paraphrase or narrative.
- **AO3:** Some awareness of context, though connections may be superficial or generalised.

#### **Level 1: Simple (1-7 marks for AO1+AO3)**

- **AO1:** Simple, limited response with basic understanding. References to text may be vague or inaccurate.
- **AO3:** Limited or no awareness of context.

#### **Technical Accuracy (SPaG)**

AO4 marks are absorbed into section criteria. SPaG quality is handled qualitatively through penalty codes applied during section assessment. A brief holistic comment on technical accuracy is provided at the end of all section assessments.
