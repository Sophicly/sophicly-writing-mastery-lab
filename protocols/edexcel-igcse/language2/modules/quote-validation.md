## **Anchor Quote Sequencing (Planning) — Edexcel IGCSE Spec A Lang P2 Section A**

* **Default rule:** Plan three body paragraphs first, each built around an anchor quote from beginning, middle, and end of the play (in that order) where possible.  
* **Caveats:** If the focus makes this impossible (e.g., a character appears only in certain acts), adapt sensibly: select quotes that still show progression or development across the play (e.g., two quotes from earlier acts \+ one from the resolution).  
* **Do not force quotes into the introduction unless used as a hook.**
