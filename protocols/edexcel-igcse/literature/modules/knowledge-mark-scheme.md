### **2.G. Official Edexcel IGCSE Mark Scheme Descriptors (June 2024\)**

**Internal AI Note:** Use these official Edexcel IGCSE level descriptors to align feedback with exam board expectations. Reference specific band characteristics when explaining marks awarded or improvements needed.

#### **Level 5: Assured knowledge and perceptive engagement (25-30 marks)**

**AO1:** Assured knowledge and understanding of the text. The response shows assured personal engagement and a perceptive critical style.

**AO2:** Cohesive evaluation of language, form and structure.

**AO4:** Understanding of the relationship between text and context is integrated convincingly into the response.

**Typical features:** Discriminating use of relevant examples in support. At the top of this level, responses demonstrate sophisticated critical analysis with assured textual knowledge, perceptive insights, and convincing contextual integration.

#### **Level 4: Thorough knowledge and sustained analysis (19-24 marks)**

**AO1:** Thorough knowledge and understanding of the text. The response shows thorough personal engagement and a sustained critical style.

**AO2:** Sustained analysis of language, form and structure.

**AO4:** There is a detailed awareness of the relationship between text and context.

**Typical features:** Use of fully relevant examples in support. Responses maintain consistent analytical depth with detailed contextual awareness throughout.

#### **Level 3: Sound knowledge and appropriate engagement (13-18 marks)**

**AO1:** Sound knowledge and understanding of the text. The response shows relevant personal engagement and an appropriate critical style.

**AO2:** Sound understanding of language, form and structure.

**AO4:** There is relevant comment on the relationship between text and context.

**Typical features:** Use of clearly relevant examples in support. Responses demonstrate secure understanding with appropriate analytical approach.

#### **Level 2: Some knowledge and some critical engagement (7-12 marks)**

**AO1:** Some knowledge and understanding of the text. The response may be largely narrative with some evidence of personal engagement or critical style.

**AO2:** Some comment on the language, form and structure.

**AO4:** There is some comment on the relationship between text and context.

**Typical features:** Some use of relevant examples in support. Responses show developing understanding but may be narrative in approach.

#### **Level 1: Limited knowledge and simple response (1-6 marks)**

**AO1:** Limited knowledge and understanding of the text. The response is simple with little evidence of personal engagement or critical style.

**AO2:** Minimal identification of language, form and structure.

**AO4:** There is little comment on the relationship between text and context.

**Typical features:** Limited use of relevant examples in support. Responses show basic awareness but lack analytical depth.
