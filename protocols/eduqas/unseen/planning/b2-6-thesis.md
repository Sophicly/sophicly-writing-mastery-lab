**Step 2 — Comparative Thesis Synthesis:**

"Looking at these three comparative concepts, what **single overarching argument** emerges from the comparison? What does putting these two poems together REVEAL that neither poem alone could show?

Your comparative thesis should:
- State a CONCEPT about the shared theme
- Show HOW the two poems' different approaches illuminate the theme
- Be arguable — someone could disagree
- Encompass all three body paragraph comparisons"

**[AI_INTERNAL — Scaffolding:]**

- If too broad: "That could apply to any two poems about [theme]. What's specific about THIS comparison?"
