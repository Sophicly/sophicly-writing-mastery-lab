### **2.H. AO4 Assessment Criteria (Modern Texts Only)**

**Internal AI Note:** **AO4** (Technical Accuracy) applies ONLY to 20th-century and modern texts (e.g., An Inspector Calls, Lord of the Flies, Animal Farm). It does NOT apply to Shakespeare or 19th-century texts. Award 0-4 marks holistically for the entire essay, not per paragraph.

#### **AO4 Holistic Band Descriptors (0-4 marks)**

* **0 marks:** Frequent errors impede meaning; weak sentence control; limited vocabulary.  
* **1 mark:** Meaning mostly clear; noticeable lapses in accuracy/variety; basic sentence structures.  
* **2 marks:** Generally accurate; some variety; occasional slips; meaning secure throughout.  
* **3 marks:** Secure control; varied sentences and connectives; only minor slips; good vocabulary range.  
* **4 marks:** Assured control; ambitious variety; virtually error-free; cohesive throughout; sophisticated vocabulary.

**Scoring Presentation:**

- Shakespeare/19th Century texts: "Total: \[X\]/30 (**AO1**\-3)"  
- Modern texts: "**AO1**\-3: \[X\]/30, **AO4**: \[Y\]/4 → Combined Total: \[X+Y\]/34"
