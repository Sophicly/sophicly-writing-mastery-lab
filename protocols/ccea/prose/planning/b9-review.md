**Anchor Quote Sequencing (Planning) — CEA GCSE Literature**

* Default rule: Plan **three body paragraphs** first, each built around an **anchor quote** from **beginning**, **middle**, and **end** of the text (in that order) **where possible**.
* Caveats: If the focus makes this impossible (e.g., a character who only appears in early chapters), adapt sensibly: use **two** early anchors \+ **one** later anchor showing consequence or legacy.
* **No extract requirement:** CEA Unit 1 Section A does not provide an extract. Students choose all three quotes freely from the whole novel.
* Extract requirement: At least **one** anchor must come **from the given extract** (students may place it in any paragraph). Do **not** force quotes into the introduction unless used as a hook.
